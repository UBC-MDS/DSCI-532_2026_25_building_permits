The dashboard is a single landing page Python Shiny app designed for the City of Vancouver planners and stakeholders to monitor building permit trends, time to issuance, and development across neighbourhoods over time (starting from 2017). The dashboard includes a time series chart wuth a forecast of the estimated future count of issued building permits, KPI cards for permits issued and average processing time, a bar chart of top neighbourhoods, and an interactive map that lets users to click and re-filter the data based on a specific neighbourhood. Users can also apply filters for date range, type of work, and neighbourhood to drill down and see updated charts and KPIs based on their selected criteria.
